About Me


Hello, my name is Roy Chee.
Welcome to my FinTech, FitTech and FunTech journey !

I am currently working in the FinTech industry and pursuing a FinTech course with National University of Singapore. I have worked on a variety of FinTech projects, which include data analysis, visualization, and implementing machine learning pipelines. I have showcased some of my projects here, and link them to their GitHub repositories.

When I'm not doing FinTech, I will embark on FitTech. I have set up a Cryptofit website to encourage people to exercise. My goal is to help you be the best that you can be and train you using scientifically proven methods and data analytics to track progress. I believe in FinTech and encourage you to use cryptocurrencies as a form of payment. I am looking at partnering with NUS-FinTech Lab and International Bitcoin Association to drive this idea.

Besides FinTech and FitTech, I normally have some time for FunTech too. During my free time, I like to code and play online games, which help to sharpen my mind and improve my problem solving skills. I have started to develop online games for fun. Feel free to try out the games here and give your comments.

Last but not least, I would like to contribute by creating some simple tutorials on a variety of data science tools to encourage people to embrace digital technologies. These tutorials are for beginners just starting out in the field. I have added links to some of my tutorials here. You can also view the professional certifications that I have attained.

Please continue to join me in my FinTech, FitTech and FunTech journey !
